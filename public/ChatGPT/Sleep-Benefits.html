Why Sleep is Healthy

\
\
\

Getting enough sleep is essential for overall health and well-being. Here are some reasons why:

\
    \
  • Sleep helps repair and rejuvenate the body, which can lead to better physical health and immune function.
    • \
  • Getting enough sleep can improve mood, reduce stress, and increase cognitive function.
    • \
  • Poor sleep is linked to an increased risk of obesity, diabetes, heart disease, and other health problems.
    • \
  • Experts recommend adults aim for 7-9 hours of sleep per night for optimal health benefits.
    • \
